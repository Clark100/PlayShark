How to Play?

Each PlayShark card has a card number, appetite and rarity.

- The card number of each PlayShark is unqiue (from No.001 to NO.1000).

- Appetite of each type of shark may be different, and it will be used in Shark Shark Arena. Generally, a higher appetite value means a stronger shark card.

- Rarity of the card from low to high is:
          Bronze(50%) -> Silver(33%) -> Gold(15%) -> Diamond(2%)
